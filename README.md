# Adidas U.S. Sales Analysis 
## Project Objective
Using 2020–2021 data to analyze Adidas’s performance and identify which areas drive the most revenue, broken down by key factors such as product category and retailer.
The goal is to generate business insights on where Adidas excels and where there is room for improvement.

## Project Description 
Sales analysis is crucial for large corporations like Adidas. Pre-processing and analyzing large datasets help evaluate KPIs (Key Performance Indicators), uncover key metrics, and build dashboards that visualize trends, sales performance, and profit margins.

## Key Findings & Insights
**Sales By Product**
-  Description: Visualized using a bar chart showing total sales by product category.
-  Insights: Men’s Street Footwear generated the highest sales, followed by Women’s Apparel. This suggests Adidas is strongly positioned in footwear and may have a slightly stronger appeal to male consumers.

**Sales By Method**
-  Description: Displayed using a pie chart showing sales distribution by sales method.
-  Insights: In-store purchases generated the most sales, with online coming second. This may indicate that in-store experiences or availability drive higher purchasing, while implying online and outlet methods may offer more discounted sales. 

**Sales By Retailer** 
-  Description: A funnel chart ranks retailers from highest to lowest sales.
-  Insights: The top three retailers are primarily sports or shoe stores, suggesting Adidas performs best in athletic-focused retail environments.

**Sales By State**
-  Description: A map chart highlights sales by state, with darker shades indicating higher sales.
-  Insights: California recorded the highest sales, likely due to its large population and active lifestyle culture. New York also showed strong performance, potentially influenced by high foot traffic and urban demand.
 
**Sales By Month**
-  Description: A line chart illustrates monthly sales trends.
-  Insights: Sales peak during the holiday season and summer months, indicating strong seasonal demand patterns.
